Introduction

Hey guys, I’m Mathmagician10. I am an amateur caster who is looking to use my statistics degree for more than just my day job, so I created this team analysis in R with what feels like a million datasets from DatDota. I hope you guys enjoy what you read, as I’m hoping this makes the draft stage a little more fun for you guys to watch since you could have a better idea of what is coming. I’ll take any and all suggestions/criticisms as I would love to know what I can do to be better. For today’s analysis, we have the OMEGA League: Americas Divine Division

This shows the average gametime in minutes for each of the teams in the tournament. The winrate for each team is scaled on the right by color, and shows a decent bell curve for the winrates of all teams.

 

The Top 3

The following data will be split into three sections, one for each phase. The goal will be to determine, solely based on statistics and not knowing the other picks/bans leading up to that phase of the draft. Inherently, this makes the first phase draft data the most impactful as you will likely dedicate one of your first bans to taking away high priority heroes on the other team, which typically are not in reaction to the other bans you would have already seen.
