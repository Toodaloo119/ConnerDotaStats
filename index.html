Twitch

I will be casting DotA 2 games evenings after work (Monday-Thursday after 4pm EST), as I am still learning on how to be better at casting, observing, and commentating. I would greatly appreciate anyone tuning in, and please feel free to give me your feedback as my number one goal is to improve.
